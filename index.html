About FinCrime Simulator

Built to give aspiring compliance professionals a realistic AML/KYC practice surface.

Why this exists

Aspiring AML/KYC analysts, CAMS candidates, and law/finance students rarely get access to real bank compliance platforms before their first role. That gap makes interviews and the first 90 days harder than they need to be.

FinCrime Simulator gives you a safe space to reason through CDD files, triage alerts, assign risk ratings, write escalation notes, and translate typologies into SQL, all using fictional scenarios built in the shape of real work.

What it is not

  • Not a real AML / KYC system
  • Not connected to any live sanctions or PEP list
  • Not a substitute for official CAMS / ICA training
  • Not legal, regulatory, or compliance advice

All customers, transactions, jurisdictions, and companies in this app are fictional. Any resemblance to a real person or entity is unintended.
